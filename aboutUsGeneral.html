ABOUT US

We are a company located in Los Angeles, CA. We take great pride in connecting fans all over the world with their favorite celebrities through facetime or skype.

KEY STARS is the platform that allows us to connect fans to celebrities through bidding competitions.

The way KEY STARS is set up is that once you create an account you can search for your favorite celebrities.

Celebrities will set aside an hour of their time to chat with the winning fans through either facetime or skype.

Fans will compete with each other in bidding competitions for a five minute block of time with their celebrities.

Once the bidding competition is over, the winning fan has 15 minutes to pay the winning bid.

If a winning fan fails to pay their winning bid by the end of the fifteen minutes, the next biggest bid has a chance to take over the block of time with the celebrity.
